Ticketing and Event FAQ


When do tickets go on Sale? A limited amount of tickets will go on sale from August 30, 2019 till September 13, 2019 at a discounted price. Later the tickets will be on sale till October 13th 2019.

How do I get tickets? To attend TEDxVVCE you just need to purchase a ticket. Unlike TED Talks, we do not have an application process to attend.

Are tickets refundable? No, Sorry once the ticket is brought it cannot be refundable.

Are tickets transferable? No, tickets are not transferable.

Do I need to print my ticket? No, save a tree and printer hassle. All TEDxVVCE tickets are electronic. Just bring yourself and your ID and we’ll do the rest.

Does each attendee have to have a ticket? Yes. Every attendee must have a ticket.

Where do I park? VVCE Campus has an ample parking space. You can park your vehicle inside the campus.

Does my ticket include lunch? Yes! It also includes coffee throughout the day.
