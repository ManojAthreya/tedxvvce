Anjali B Gupta

Kick Boxer


Read More

Mother of 2/ Business Owner/ Krateka/ Kickboxer/ MMA Fighter/ Powerlifter Anjali lives to break stereotypes. Brought up in Mysore, her earliest memories are of riding horses with the Mysore Mounted Police. She excelled in volleyball, kho-kho & throwball, and won Silver in Kabaddi at the State Dasara Sports in 1996. She moved to the US and worked while going to college in the evenings and weekends, in New York City. With the birth of her children and with the tugs of being a full time mom, she threw herself into learning the Tabla, and Ukulele, mixing songs on Garageband, resulting in a few re-imagined Shlokas on YouTube. Once back, she found herself single with her children, overweight, and depressed, while having to deal with the stresses of Indian life that she hadn't been a part of for over a decade. Not being one for "gymming", a friend suggested MMA. In September 2014, she stepped into her first MMA class, and the rest, is History.

Author

Christopher C Doyle is a bestselling author who transports the reader into a fascinating world where ancient secrets buried in legends blend with science and history to create a gripping story. His first three novels – The Mahabharata Secret (released in 2013), The Mahabharata Quest: The Alexander Secret (released in 2014) and The Mahabharata Quest: The Secret of the Druids (released in 2016) – explore the science behind the enduring legends of the Mahabharata, in an explosive blend of science, mythology, history and action and are all bestsellers. His debut novel, The Mahabharata Secret, was also nominated for the Raymond Crossword Book Award, 2014, as one of the top 12 books in the popular category. Over the course of his corporate career, Christopher has written articles on management and business for Indian and international publications and is also a regular invited speaker for international conventions and conferences. He is also a musician and lives his passion for music through his band which plays classic rock.

Sudhir Vombatkere

Campaigner for a sustainable future


Read More

Sudhir Vombatkere is a soldier, engineer, cross-discipline thinker, writer, fitness enthusiast, optimist and a firm believer in the Constitution of India. He is recipient of the Visistha Seva Medal from President of India in 1994, for distinguished military service. He was responsible for design and construction of a motorable bridge founded on ice at an altitude of 5,602-m in Ladakh in 1982. He is the only Indian engineer to earn a mention in the Guinness Book of Records and the Limca Book of Records. As Adjunct Associate Professor in International Studies of the University of Iowa, USA, he was teaching a course on Sustainable Development to undergraduate students from US & Canadian universities. He holds a PhD in Civil Structural Engineering from I.I.T., Madras (1987). Since his retirement in the rank of Major General in 1996, he has been engaged in voluntary public service.
